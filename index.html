About Mabini

Mabini, located in the province of Batangas, is a serene coastal town celebrated for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life. Known as the diving capital of the Philippines, it offers world-class dive sites, pristine beaches, and breathtaking sunsets. With its laid-back atmosphere and natural beauty, Mabini is the ideal escape for travelers seeking relaxation, adventure, and a true tropical paradise.
